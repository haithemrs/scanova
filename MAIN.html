Frequently Asked Questions

What lung diseases can Scanova detect?

Scanova's AI detects conditions like:

  • Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)
  • Asthma
  • Pneumonia
  • Lung Cancer
  • Tuberculosis
  • Pulmonary Fibrosis

Early detection helps you take control of your health.

How accurate is Scanova's AI?

Our AI achieves over 95% accuracy in identifying key lung conditions. This high accuracy is the result of training on thousands of validated medical scans and continuous refinement by leading pulmonologists. However, we always recommend consulting with a healthcare professional for a final diagnosis.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We employ industry-leading encryption standards to protect your medical data and personal information. Our platform is fully HIPAA-compliant, ensuring your information is treated with the highest level of privacy and security protocols.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No referral is needed to use Scanova. Simply upload your existing lung scan and get results directly. However, if you don't have a recent scan, we can help schedule one through our network of imaging partners.

How long for results?

Results are delivered instantly for all our users. Our advanced AI processes your scan quickly, while maintaining the highest standards of accuracy.

Can I book appointments?

Yes, Premium members can schedule consultations with lung health specialists directly through our platform. We connect you with qualified professionals who can discuss your scan results and recommend next steps.
